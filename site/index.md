## Venue

We will be in EC1-29 for the whole course. Don't know where that is? Go to the Frank Whittle Building (used to be called the engineering building) and go up the stairs on the south side near the entrance and reception. 

Please bring your laptop and power supply. We will try to accommodate access to power sockets on the day. 

## Photography

We will be taking some photos of the event. Consent forms will be handed out during the sessions prior to any photography. Please feel free to say that you don't want your picture taken. 

## Speakers
